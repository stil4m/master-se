P vs. NP

  • Nondeterministic Polynomial time
SAT solving for propositional logic
  • Given $\varphi$ is there a $V$ that makes $\varphi$ true? (NP)
  • Given $\varphi$ and $V$, does make $V$ $\varphi$ true? (P)
